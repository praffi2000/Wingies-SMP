Desert biomes are usually vast, quite barren, and lifeless, with the only vegetation being cacti, and dead bushes. Among the mobs that can spawn in the desert, only rabbits spawn in the wild, while villagers, cats, cows, sheep, and pigs appear in desert villages. The terrain is flat and the surface is made entirely of sand, with sandstone generating underneath the sand to prevent it from falling into caves below. Water ponds do not generate in deserts aside from the Desert Lakes variant, though lava ponds may still generate.
